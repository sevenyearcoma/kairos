Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

01. Introduction

Kairos ("we," "our," or "us") is a "Kind AI Responsible for Organizing Schedule." We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Kairos collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our web application.

Our core philosophy is Local-First. We do not operate a centralized database that stores your personal calendar events, tasks, or chat history. Your data resides on your device and is processed via secure APIs.

02. Information We Collect & Use

We collect information to provide the scheduling and assistant features of the application.

Google Calendar Data

Scope: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events

We access your calendar to display your schedule, check for conflicts, and create new events based on your chat requests. We do not store this data on our servers; it is fetched directly to your browser.

Google Tasks Data

Scope: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/tasks

We access your tasks to organize your to-do list within the "Eisenhower Matrix" view and to sync completion status.

Local AI Context

We store a local "Knowledge Base" (preferences, work habits, name) in your browser's Local Storage to give the AI context. This data is never sent to a third-party database other than the specific AI model for processing the immediate request.

03. Limited Use Disclosure

Kairos's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We do not use Google Workspace data for training generalized AI models. The data is processed ephemerally to generate a response for your specific session.

04. AI Processing (Gemini)

To provide conversational capabilities, text input and relevant context (such as your schedule for the day) are sent to Google's Gemini API.

  • check_circle Data is sent strictly for the purpose of generating a response (e.g., "Schedule a meeting").
  • check_circle We do not sell your personal data or conversation history.

05. Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data.

A

Revoke Google Access: via your Google Security Settings.

B

Delete Local Data: Clear your browser's "Local Storage" or cache.
